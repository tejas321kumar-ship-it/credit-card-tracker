Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

Is my financial data safe?
Absolutely! We use bank-grade encryption and never store your full card number or CVV. Only the last 4 digits are saved for identification. Your data is stored locally and never shared with third parties.
Is Card Tracker really free?
Yes! Card Tracker is completely free to use. There are no hidden charges, premium tiers, or ads. We believe everyone deserves great financial management tools.
Can I track multiple credit cards?
Yes! You can add and manage as many credit cards as you need. Switch between them using our beautiful card carousel on the dashboard.
Does it support UPI payments?
Yes! You can send money via UPI directly from the dashboard. It generates UPI deep links and QR codes compatible with GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, and all other UPI apps.
What devices does it work on?
Card Tracker works on any device with a web browser — desktop, tablet, or mobile. It's also installable as a Progressive Web App (PWA) for a native-like experience on your phone.
